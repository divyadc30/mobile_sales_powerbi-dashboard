 Mobile Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

This project is an interactive **Power BI dashboard** created to analyze mobile phone sales data across different cities, brands, payment methods, and months.
The dashboard provides insights into total sales, quantity sold, customer ratings, transactions, and brand performance using multiple visualizations.

---
Dashboard Overview

The dashboard includes the following key insights:

• Total Sales: 769M  
• Total Quantity Sold: 19K  
• Total Transactions: 4K  
• Average Price: 40K  

---

📈 Dashboard Visualizations

The report contains the following visuals:

1️ **Total Sales by City (Map Chart)**  
Shows the distribution of mobile sales across major cities in India.

2️ **Units Sold by Month (Line Chart)**  
Displays monthly sales trends to identify peak sales periods.

3️ **Total Sales by Mobile Model (Bar Chart)**  
Compares sales of different mobile models such as iPhone, OnePlus, and Galaxy.

4️ **Transactions by Payment Method (Pie Chart)**  
Shows the percentage of payments done through:
- UPI
- Debit Card
- Credit Card
- Cash

5️ **Customer Ratings (Bar Chart)**  
Displays the distribution of customer ratings from 1 to 5.

6️ **Total Sales by Brand (Donut Chart)**  
Shows brand-wise sales distribution among:
- Apple
- Samsung
- Xiaomi
- OnePlus
- Vivo

7️ **Total Sales by Day Name (Line Chart)**  
Shows which days generate the highest sales.

8️ **Month Filter (Slicer)**  
Allows users to filter dashboard data by month.

 Key Insights

• Apple and Samsung contribute a large share of total sales.  
• UPI and Debit Card are the most used payment methods.

• Higher customer ratings are more common, showing good customer satisfaction.
